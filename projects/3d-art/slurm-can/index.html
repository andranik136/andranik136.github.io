Can of Slurm

August 2025
Blender

This project is a 3D tribute to the highly addictive beverage from the world of Futurama—Slurm. The goal was to create a compelling, commercial-style shot that brings the fictional product to life.

The entire scene was modeled, textured, and rendered in Blender. A key technical focus was the complex fluid simulation used to create the energetic green splash, frozen at the perfect moment of impact. The can's material features a metallic finish with custom-designed textures inspired by the original show, while the lighting is set up to highlight the liquid's translucency and the can's reflections.
