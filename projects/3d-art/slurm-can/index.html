Slurm Can

August 2025
Blender

This project is a 3D visualization inspired by Slurm, the iconic soda from Futurama. Using Blender, I created a dynamic scene where a Slurm can is dramatically splashed by vibrant green liquid. To achieve a realistic and energetic effect, I leveraged Blender’s fluid simulation tools to model the splash and flow of the liquid, ensuring a natural sense of motion and weight. To enhance the visual impact, I applied studio lighting techniques that highlight the crisp metallic surface of the can and the translucency of the liquid. The bright, polished lighting setup gives the scene a clean, commercial look—similar to modern product photography—while the playful subject matter keeps it fun and eye-catching. This project was a great exercise in combining simulation, lighting, and rendering to produce a lively, high-quality 3D still inspired by pop culture.
