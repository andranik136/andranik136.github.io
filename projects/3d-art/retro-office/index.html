Retro Office

October 2024
Blender

This Blender project was a dive into creating a space that feels utterly lived-in and full of history: the quintessential old-school game room. I wanted to capture that distinct grungy, cozy, and slightly forgotten atmosphere where countless hours were spent.

The challenge was in the details â€“ not just modeling the core furniture like the chunky TV, and the coffee table, but in meticulously texturing everything to show its age. From the peeling wallpaper and the dusty CRT screen playing a PS2 classic, to the stack of games and old magazines, every element contributes to the narrative. The lighting, with its distinct shadows from the window blinds, really ties the whole nostalgic vibe together. This piece was a joy to create, focusing on texture work and environmental storytelling to build a believable, atmospheric scene.
