Folding Knife

August 2025
Blender

This Blender project served as an intensive study in achieving material realism and controlled studio illumination for hard-surface product rendering. The subject, a folding knife, provided an excellent opportunity to explore nuanced material interactions and precise lighting.

My workflow involved detailed hard-surface modeling to create an accurate base mesh. The subsequent emphasis was on material development: crafting complex shader networks for the blade's brushed steel, the handle's textured wood, and the various metallic components. Each material was calibrated using PBR principles to ensure accurate light interaction. The final presentation was refined using a multi-point studio lighting rig, carefully positioned to define edges, reveal surface imperfections, and create a strong, professional product aesthetic.
